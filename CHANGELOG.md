## How to subscribe

- **Auto-updater** — Settings → About → "Check for updates" (or the periodic background check, on by default).
- **GitHub Watch** — top-right of this repo → Watch → Releases only.
- **RSS** — `https://github.com/AzmxAI/azmx/releases.atom`.

## Versioning

AZMX follows [semver](https://semver.org):

- **Patch** (0.22.x) — bug fixes, polish, dependency bumps.
- **Minor** (0.23.0) — backward-compatible features.
- **Major** (1.0) — only when the public surface stabilizes for real.
